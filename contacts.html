Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling your car to us. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

How long is my quote valid for?

Your instant valuation is valid for 7 days from the date of issue. This gives you time to compare offers, think it over, or arrange your next vehicle. Once you accept the offer, we lock the price in and it won't change — even if market conditions shift.

Do you really buy any car in any condition?

Yes — absolutely. We buy cars that are damaged, written off, non-runners, scrap, MOT failures, high mileage, old, new, leased, financed, and everything in between. If it has wheels and an engine, we'll make you an offer. The only vehicles we cannot purchase are those with outstanding finance that exceeds the vehicle's value (though we can often still help by negotiating with your lender).

How quickly do I get paid?

Instantly. On collection day, our driver inspects the vehicle to confirm it matches your description. Assuming everything checks out, we initiate payment immediately. Bank transfers typically clear within 2 hours (same day if before 3pm). We also offer cash or cheque if you prefer. You'll receive a receipt and bill of sale before our driver leaves.

Is collection really free?

Yes — collection is completely free, with no hidden charges. We collect from anywhere in the UK: England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Whether you're in central London or the Scottish Highlands, we'll come to you at no cost. We can collect from your home, workplace, garage, or even a roadside breakdown location.

What documents do I need?

You'll need your V5C logbook (vehicle registration document) and a valid form of photo ID (driving licence or passport). If your vehicle has outstanding finance, we'll also need a settlement letter from your lender. We handle all DVLA paperwork for you — just sign the V5C and we'll notify them of the change of ownership.

My car has outstanding finance — can I still sell it?

In most cases, yes. We can settle the outstanding finance directly with your lender and pay you any remaining equity. If the settlement amount exceeds the car's value, you'll need to pay the difference (negative equity). We'll guide you through this process step by step. Just let us know about the finance when submitting your quote.

What if the vehicle condition is worse than I described?

Honesty is the best policy. We understand that not everyone is a mechanic, so minor discrepancies are fine. However, if the condition is significantly worse than described (e.g., you said "good" but the car has major structural damage), we may need to adjust our offer. Our driver will discuss any changes with you on collection day, and you're free to decline the revised offer with no penalty.

Do you buy vans and commercial vehicles?

Absolutely. We buy vans, pickups, minibuses, Luton vans, tipper trucks, and all types of commercial vehicles. Fleet disposals are also welcome — if you have multiple vehicles to sell, contact us for a bespoke fleet valuation service.

Can I cancel after accepting the offer?

Yes — you can cancel at any time before collection with no penalty. Simply call us on 0800 123 4567 or email quotes@webuyanycar.co.uk with your quote reference number. There are no cancellation fees, no admin charges, and no hard feelings.

How do you calculate the valuation?

Our valuation engine pulls data from CAP, Glass's Guide, and live auction results to calculate a fair market price. We factor in the vehicle's make, model, year, mileage, condition, fuel type, and current market demand. Unlike some competitors, we don't use automated lowball algorithms — every quote is reviewed by a human before being sent to you.

Do you buy left-hand drive or imported cars?

Yes, we buy left-hand drive vehicles and imported cars. Please mention this in the "Additional Information" section when submitting your quote, as it may affect the valuation. We'll need to see the vehicle's import documentation on collection day.

What areas of the UK do you cover?

We cover the entire United Kingdom — England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Our collection network includes drivers in every major city and most towns. Even if you're in a remote rural location, we'll arrange collection. There are no additional charges for remote areas.
