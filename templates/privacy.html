This Privacy Policy explains how WeBuyAnyVehicle UK Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identity Data: Your name, title, and date of birth
  • Contact Data: Your address, email address, and telephone number
  • Vehicle Data: Make, model, year, mileage, registration number, condition, and photographs of your vehicle
  • Financial Data: Bank account details (only when processing payment)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website and services

2. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data through:

  • Direct interactions — when you fill in forms, submit a quote request, or contact us
  • Automated technologies — cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Third parties — DVLA (for vehicle verification) and credit reference agencies (for finance settlement)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide our vehicle purchasing services and process your quote
  • To arrange vehicle collection and payment
  • To communicate with you about your quote, collection, and payment
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., DVLA notification, anti-money laundering)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil our contract with you (buying your vehicle)
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required by law (DVLA notification, tax compliance)
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing for our legitimate business interests (fraud prevention, service improvement)
  • Consent: Processing based on your explicit consent (marketing communications)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • DVLA — for vehicle ownership transfer and verification
  • Payment processors — to facilitate bank transfers
  • Collection partners — to arrange vehicle pickup
  • Legal and regulatory authorities — when required by law
  • Professional advisers — accountants, lawyers, insurers

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular staff training on data protection
  • Data retention limits — we keep your data only as long as necessary

7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organisation
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior lawful processing)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@webuyanyvehicle.co.uk.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Quote and transaction records: 7 years (for tax and legal compliance)
  • Marketing data: Until you withdraw consent or unsubscribe
  • Website analytics: 26 months

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
