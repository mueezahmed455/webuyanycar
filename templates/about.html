About Us

We Buy Any Vehicle. It's Not Just a Slogan — It's What We Do.

Founded in Birmingham in 2019, webuyanyvehicle UK has grown from a one-man operation buying scrap cars from driveways to one of the UK's most trusted vehicle purchasing services. We handle everything from pristine low-mileage vehicles to Category S write-offs, and we treat every seller with the same respect and transparency.

Our Story

We started because we were tired of seeing ordinary people get ripped off. The traditional car selling process is broken — private buyers who waste your time, dealers who lowball you, scrap yards who pay pennies. We knew there was a better way.

Today, we operate a nationwide collection network with drivers in every major UK city. Our valuation engine pulls data from CAP, Glass's Guide, and live auction results to ensure you get a fair market price — not a guess.

We're not a faceless corporation. When you call us, you speak to a real person who knows cars. When we collect your vehicle, our drivers are trained, insured, and professional. And when we say instant payment, we mean it — the money is in your account before our truck leaves your street.
