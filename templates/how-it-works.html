The Process

How It Works

We've stripped away the complexity of selling a car. No haggling, no tyre-kickers, no waiting weeks for a buyer. Just five straightforward steps from quote to payment.

1

Submit Your Vehicle Details

Head to our quote form and tell us about your car. We need the basics: make, model, year, mileage, and condition. Be honest — we buy cars in every condition imaginable, so there's no need to hide anything.

You can also upload photos of your vehicle. This helps us give you the most accurate valuation possible. Show us the front, back, sides, interior, and any damage or issues.

Takes approximately 2 minutes

2

Receive Your Instant Valuation

Our valuation engine analyses your vehicle against real-time market data from CAP, Glass's Guide, and live auction results. Within 60 seconds, you'll have a firm, no-obligation quote.

The quote is valid for 7 days, giving you time to think it over or compare against other offers. We guarantee to beat any genuine quote by £100.

Takes approximately 60 seconds

3

Accept The Offer

Happy with the price? Simply accept the offer through our website or by calling us. Once accepted, the price is locked in for 7 days — even if market conditions change.

We'll send you a confirmation email with your quote reference number and next steps.

Instant confirmation

4

Arrange Free Collection

We collect from anywhere in the UK — your home, workplace, garage, or even a breakdown location. Our professional drivers are fully insured and trained to handle vehicles in any condition.

Collections are available 7 days a week, including evenings. Typical collection time is within 24-48 hours of acceptance.

Usually within 24-48 hours

5

Get Paid Instantly

On collection day, our driver will inspect the vehicle to confirm it matches your description. Assuming everything checks out, we initiate payment immediately.

Bank transfers typically clear within 2 hours. We also offer cash or cheque. We handle all DVLA paperwork — just hand over the V5C and you're done.

Money in your account within 2 hours
